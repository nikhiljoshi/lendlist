Never again forget what you borrowed from someone and keep track of what your friends borrowed from you.
Features:
* Jot down what you lent/borrowed
* Connect with your contacts
* Export due dates to calendar
* Reminder when items are due to return
* Backup/Export data
Feel free to contact raphael@geeksfactory.de and give feedback :)
This app is free and open source software, if you are interested in the source code, take a look on https://github.com/raphaelm/lendlist
